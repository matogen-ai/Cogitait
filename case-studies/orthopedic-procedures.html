Advanced Orthopaedic Analytics

COGITAIT analysed millions of records from a US database, focusing on orthopaedic procedures in patients with dismineral diseases. Using ICD9 codes and SAS software, we identified key risk factors and demographics linked to post-procedural mortality in procedures like hip and knee arthroplasty and spinal fusion, providing critical insights for improving outcomes and guiding surgical decisions.
